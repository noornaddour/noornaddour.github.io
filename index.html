US Tariffs: IEEPA tariff refunds

March 2026

US tariffs imposed under the International Emergency Economic Powers Act (IEEPA) have been ruled unlawful, opening the door for eligible importers to claim refunds.

What does the ruling mean?

The court decision challenges the legal foundation of the tariffs, potentially reshaping how emergency economic authorities are applied going forward.

“This decision could have significant implications for both historical tariff payments and future trade actions.”

Next steps for businesses

Companies should assess their import history, prepare refund claims, and monitor future regulatory or legislative responses.
